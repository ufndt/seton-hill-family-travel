Game Day Information

Seton Hill Griffins play at Dick Assad Field (1 Seton Hill Dr, Greensburg, PA 15601). Check the official schedule for 2025 game dates. Parking is available on campus; arrive early for spots.

  • Tickets: Purchase via Seton Hill’s athletics website or at the gate.
  • Tailgating: Limited spaces; check university policies.
  • Nearby Dining: Primanti Brothers (830 E Pittsburgh St), Oliver’s Pourhouse (8 N Pennsylvania Ave), or Buffalo Wild Wings (6215 US-30).
    • [](https://www.setonhill.edu/apply-afford/applying-to-seton-hill/plan-your-visit/lodging-transportation-dining.html)

Travel Tips

  • Fly into PIT for more flight options; rent a car for the 45-mile drive to Greensburg.
  • Book flights and hotels early for September–November games to save money.
  • Use Westmoreland County Transit Authority (westmorelandtransit.com) for local transport if needed.
    • [](https://www.setonhill.edu/apply-afford/applying-to-seton-hill/plan-your-visit/lodging-transportation-dining.html)
  • For large families, choose hotels with extra beds or suites (e.g., Courtyard by Marriott).
  • Explore Greensburg attractions like Westmoreland Museum or Twin Lakes Park between games.
